Location

Our wedding will be entirely contained at the Waterfront Hotel Burlington, situated at the northeast end of Spencer Smith Park. The location and address of the hotel are provided in the map below. Parking is available at the hotel but is limited. Guests overnighting at the hotel can purchase a parking pass from the front desk upon arrival for $5 per vehicle, per day. Other nearby parking locations are indicated on the map with stars.

The day will begin with the ceremony. Weather permitting, we will hold this on the hotel's lakeside patio. The patio can be accessed by walking around the southwest side of the building (pictured below). Our ushers will be available to direct you and assist if necessary.

Photos

We welcome our guests to photograph to their hearts content throughout the festivities and greatly appreciate any photos you may wish to share. However, our photographer requests that guests please refrain from using flashes during the ceremony and to respect her equipment. A happy photographer probably means better photos of our big day. Many thanks in advance for your cooperation!
